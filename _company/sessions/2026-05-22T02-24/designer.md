# ActionPlan_Final_Design_Spec
## Core Concept: Pain Point First & Prioritization Clarity (시간 및 시행착오 감소 극대화)

### 1. Visual Flow Structure
*   **Step 1: Pain Point Mapping:** Display Risk Score vs. Inputted Pain Points using a contrast heatmap. (Focus on immediate urgency).
*   **Step 2: Prioritization & Linking:** Interactive drag-and-drop linking of Tasks to Pain Points, automatically calculating ROI potential based on established criteria. (Focus on decision clarity).
*   **Step 3: Execution Roadmap:** Timeline visualization showing the optimized sequence of steps with estimated time blocks and projected ROI contribution for each task. (Focus on time saving).
*   **Step 4: Final CTA:** Clear, high-contrast action button leading to plan generation.

### 2. Core CTA Visualization Design
*   **Primary CTA ("Start Plan"):** Must incorporate a subtle visual element indicating predicted financial gain (e.g., small upward trend line graphic near the button). Color: Deep Teal (#00796B).
*   **Secondary CTA ("Review Pain Points"):** Positioned prominently for easy iteration. Color: Subtle Grey or Light Blue.

### 3. Branding Application
*   **Success/ROI:** Gold Accent (#FFC107) for all calculated positive outcomes and finalized plan elements.
*   **Risk/Pain:** Soft Red (#D32F2F) for immediate attention to risks.
*   **Typography:** Use bold, clean sans-serif fonts for all numerical data (ROI, Time estimates) to maximize data clarity and trust.
